Sharpson - Kate Bush feat. Hannah Mamalis // Music Video

Sharpson' delivers with 'Kate Bush'. Again, teaming up with director and actor 'Niall Cutler' of the Sketch Comedy Group 'Chaps Eye' & 'Glick Bate', he delivers another how we could describe as another 'out there' music video. 'Kate Bush' stars 'Hannah Mamalis', an Irish comedian and a member of the 'Dream Gun' comedy troupe who gives us a performance that suits the strange video premise fittingly. The video itself is a mash-up of all of Kate Bush's trippy music videos that has graced our screens throughout the year.
